About Adam

Adam Sandler is an American comedian known for his portrayal of infantile but endearing characters. He was born September 9, 1966, in Brooklyn, New York.Sandler was raised in Manchester, New Hampshire, the youngest of four children. Academically disinclined, he was frequently disciplined for his comedic antics in school. He first performed at a stand-up comedy club in Boston at age 17 at the urging of his brother.
